# Generative Modeling

A series of 10 Jupyter notebooks covering the theory and implementation of modern generative models, from probability foundations through advanced score-based methods.

## Assignments

| # | Topic | Key Concepts |
|---|-------|-------------|
| 1 | [Probability Basics](assignment_01_probability_basics.ipynb) | Gaussian distributions, frequentist vs Bayesian, generative vs discriminative models |
| 2 | [KL Divergence & MLE](assignment_02_kl_and_mle.ipynb) | KL divergence, Jensen's inequality, maximum likelihood estimation |
| 3 | [Gaussian Mixture Models](assignment_03_gaussian_mixture_models.ipynb) | GMMs, EM algorithm, ELBO, log-sum-exp trick |
| 4 | [PPCA & VAEs](assignment_04_ppca_and_vae.ipynb) | Probabilistic PCA, ELBO derivation, reparameterisation trick, VAE in PyTorch |
| 5 | [Energy-Based Models](assignment_05_energy_based_models.ipynb) | Gibbs distribution, partition function, MCMC, Metropolis-Hastings |
| 6 | [Diffusion Models](assignment_06_diffusion_models.ipynb) | DDPMs, noise schedules, forward/reverse process |
| 7 | [Score-Based Models](assignment_07_score_based_models.ipynb) | Score functions, score matching, denoising score matching, Langevin dynamics |
| 8 | [Normalising Flows](assignment_08_normalising_flows.ipynb) | Change of variables, affine coupling layers, RealNVP |
| 9 | [Advanced Flows](assignment_09_advanced_flows.ipynb) | Continuous normalising flows, flow matching, ODE-based generation |
| 10 | [Advanced Score-Based Models](assignment_10_score_based_models_advanced.ipynb) | Multi-scale noise, noise-conditional score networks, annealed Langevin dynamics, unifying score-based models and diffusion |

## Prerequisites

- Python 3.8+
- PyTorch
- NumPy, Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook or JupyterLab
